Cultural Etiquette

Greetings

Always say "Bonjour" when entering shops and "Au revoir" when leaving. It's considered polite and essential.

Dining

Lunch is typically 12-2pm, dinner after 7:30pm. Don't rush meals - dining is a social experience in France.

Photography

Ask permission before photographing people. Some museums allow photos, others don't - always check first.
